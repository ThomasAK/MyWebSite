Mom's Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • Tortilias
  • 1 Chicken breast
  • 1 can refried beans
  • 1 can cream of chicken
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 can red or green enchilada sauce
  • 2 cups cheddar cheese

Cooking Directions

Preheat oven to 400.
Line pan with olive oil.
Place chicken in pan then season with salt pepper and then some olive oil on top.
Cook chicken in oven for 23 minutes for smallers breats and 27 for larger breasts.
while chicken is cooking in a big bowl add together refried beans, sour cream, and cream of chicken.
Once chicken is done shred it then add it to the bowl with the other ingredients.
In a pan line bottom with enchilada sauce.
Fill tortillas with mixtuer and place in pan.
Cover in enchilada sauce then cover in cheddar cheese.
with oven still heated to 400 cook enchilads for 16 minutes.
Optional broil for 2 to 3 minutes to crisp cheese.
