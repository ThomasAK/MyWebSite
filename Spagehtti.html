Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Spaghetti Sauce your choice
  • Beer Brats
  • Parmesan cheese

Cooking Directions

Cook noodles till they are al dente.
Cook beer brats in pan or grill till they are cook to the disired done ness!
Heat pasta sauce.
Then throw it all together on a plate and top with parm cheese and any other sides you desire.
