Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine Noodles
  • 2 Chicken breasts
  • 1 Stick butter
  • 2 Cups parm cheese
  • 1 Cup heavy cream
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt & Pepper

Cooking Directions

Preheat oven to 400.
Line pan with olive oil.
Place chicken in pan then season with salt pepper and garlic powder and then some olive oil on top.
Cook chicken in oven for 23 minutes for smallers breats and 27 for larger breasts.
Cook noodles till they are al dente.
When there is about 8 minutes left on the chicken start the alfredo sauce.
Melt the stick of butter in a pan and add the 1 cup of heave cream. Then season with pepper and garlic powder only. Bring to boil then slowly add in 1 1/2 parmesan.
As the cheese melts it will become less thick. Make sure to stir constantly. Once the sauce has no sign of cheese clumps it is done.
Put noodles on plate then add sauce and top with chicken and extra parmesan.
